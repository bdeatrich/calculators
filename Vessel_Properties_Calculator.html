Flow per vessel = total flow ÷ duty trains (one flow path per train). HLR = flow per vessel ÷ bed area; EBCT = bed volume (gal) ÷ flow per vessel, shown as EBCT / train (a lead/lag pair in series gives twice the per-vessel value). Lead/lag adds a duplicate lag vessel in series per train, so media volume, media mass and bed volume double, and effective bed depth doubles, but filter area does not (the same cross-section sets HLR). Freeboard = straight wall depth − expanded bed depth; it turns red if the expanded bed exceeds the wall. Media volume = bed depth × area; density lb/cf = g/cc × 62.428.
HLR = Q/A · EBCT = BV gal / Q · bed depth = media ÷ density ÷ area · expanded = depth × (1 + expansion)
